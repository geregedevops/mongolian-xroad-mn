/etc/xroad tree on cs.gerege.mn (snapshot 2026-04-22)

Layout that a fresh `xroad-centralserver` install produces, with the
Mongolia-specific bits annotated. Secret-bearing subtrees (gpghome,
signer/) are listed here for navigation only — no files from them
live in this repo.
